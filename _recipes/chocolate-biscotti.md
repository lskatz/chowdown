ingredients:
  - 4oz bittersweet chocolate, broken into pieces and melted
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 1/2 cup oil
  - 2 eggs
  - 3 Tbsp vanilla sugar (1 tsp vanilla, 3 Tbsp sugar)
  - 1/2 unsweetened cocoa
  - 1 Tbsp potato starch (or flour or tapioca starch)
  - 1 1/2 cups ground almonds (coffee grinder)
  - 1/4 tsp salt 
  - 1 Cup chocolate chips–semi-sweet
directions:
  - Preheat oven to 350
  - line cookie sheet with parchment paper
  - Combine the first 5 ingredients
  - Add cocoa
  - Combine 7, 8, & 9
  - Add in chocolate chips
  - Divide dough in half and make into 2 loaves, each 9” X 3”.  Bake 30 minutes, let cool 10 minutes
  - Cut loaf crosswise into 3/4 or 1 inch.  Place cookies on their side and bake 14 minutes.  Check at 10-12 minutes
